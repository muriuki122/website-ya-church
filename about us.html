Israelite Ceremonies We Observe

As part of our spiritual practice, we remember and observe certain ceremonies that connect us to our biblical roots.

Passover

We commemorate the Exodus from Egypt and reflect on Jesus as our Passover Lamb who was sacrificed for us.

Observed in Spring

Pentecost (Shavuot)

We celebrate the giving of the Torah at Mount Sinai and the outpouring of the Holy Spirit in the New Testament.

50 days after Passover

Feast of Trumpets

A day of blowing trumpets and spiritual preparation, looking forward to the return of Christ.

Autumn observance

Feast of Tabernacles

We remember Israel's wilderness journey and look forward to the Kingdom of God when He will dwell with us.
